To forward your emails to another email address, follow the steps below:

1. Click Create a filter (next to the Search the Web button at the top of any AUCmail page).

2. Enter your email address in the “To:” field, as shown below.

3. Click Next Step.

4. Select “Forward it to” from the actions list and enter the address to forward the emails to.



6. Click Create Filter.




Note: To forward your emails to more than one email address, please repeat the instructions above for each email address.
