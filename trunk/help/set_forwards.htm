Setting e-mail forward:

1. Sign in to AUCmail (http://mail.aucegypt.edu)

2. Click Settings at the top of any AUCmail page, and open the Forwarding and POP/IMAP tab.

3. Enter the email address to which you'd like your messages forwarded.

4. Select the action you'd like your messages to take from the drop-down menu. You can choose to keep AUCmail's copy of the message in your inbox, or you can send it automatically to All Mail (i.e. archive it) or Trash.


5. Click Save Changes.
